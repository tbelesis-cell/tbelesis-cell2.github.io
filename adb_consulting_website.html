Who We Are

Deep Expertise.
Proven Delivery.

ADB Consulting is a boutique US-registered advisory firm specializing in the global regulated gaming industry. Led by Anastasios (Tasos) Belesis — a senior executive with over three decades of hands-on leadership across national lottery operations, VLT product management, iGaming launch, and betting regulation — ADB brings operator-grade expertise to every mandate.

Our engagements span concession setup, omnichannel game launches, regulatory compliance, operational turnarounds, P&L optimization, and go-to-market strategy. We work with operators, government bodies, technology suppliers, and investors who require structured thinking, deep domain knowledge, and dependable execution.

ADB Consulting operates across jurisdictions, advising clients in Southern Europe, East Africa, the Middle East, and beyond — combining global perspective with on-the-ground market insight.
